About Kol-Kobja

Kol-Kobja Gallery is a Kolkata-inspired art brand that blends creativity, culture, and craftsmanship. Our most popular products — posters — are known for their bold storytelling and unique visual identity.

Beyond posters, we explore paintings, books, magazines, cards, bookmarks, and handcrafted creations. Each piece reflects the essence of the city — its streets, literature, rhythm, and soul.

We aim to make art accessible, meaningful, and rooted in the visual heritage of Bengal and India.
